Uji Matcha Cheesecake Tarte Workshop
The Uji Matcha Cheesecake Tarte workshop is a delightful and stylish experience, perfect for those who are passionate about baking and love the unique combination of rich matcha flavor and smooth, creamy cheesecake.
Throughout the session, you will be carefully guided on how to make a crispy, fragrant tart crust, along with a silky-smooth matcha cheesecake filling that is not overly sweet but carries the distinctive, refreshing flavor of Uji matcha from Japan. Especially, you will be guided on creative ways to decorate your tart, from the smooth layers of matcha to the small, beautiful details that complete the delicious and visually stunning dessert.
